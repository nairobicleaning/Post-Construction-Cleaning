# Post-construction Cleaning in Nairobi

Post-build environments are often left with heavy dust, debris, paint splashes, and construction residue that require specialized cleaning before occupancy. **Post-construction Cleaning in Nairobi** is a critical final step in any building or renovation project, ensuring spaces are safe, hygienic, and ready for use. From residential homes to commercial complexes, thorough cleaning enhances presentation, protects finishes, and supports compliance with health standards.

## What Post-construction Cleaning Involves

**Post-construction Cleaning in Nairobi** goes beyond standard cleaning. It targets fine dust, adhesives, cement residues, and protective films left behind after construction. The process is typically divided into phases to achieve a polished, move-in-ready result.

### Phases of Post-construction Cleaning

1. **Rough Cleaning**

   * Removal of large debris and waste materials
   * Sweeping and vacuuming of floors
   * Clearing packaging, offcuts, and rubble

2. **Detailed Cleaning**

   * Dusting of all surfaces including ceilings, walls, and fixtures
   * Cleaning windows, frames, and tracks
   * Removing paint splashes, grout haze, and stickers

3. **Final Touch Cleaning**

   * Deep cleaning of floors and carpets
   * Sanitization of kitchens and bathrooms
   * Polishing fixtures and fittings

This structured approach ensures consistent outcomes in **Post-construction Cleaning in Nairobi** projects.

## Core Services Included

### Debris and Waste Removal

Construction sites accumulate leftover materials that must be safely removed and disposed of according to local regulations.

### Dust Extraction

Fine dust settles on every surface, including vents and light fixtures. Industrial vacuums and microfiber systems are used to eliminate airborne particles.

### Window and Glass Cleaning

Removal of paint marks, stickers, and cement residues from glass surfaces restores clarity and improves aesthetics.

### Floor Cleaning and Polishing

Different floor types require tailored methods, including scrubbing, buffing, and sealing to protect surfaces.

### Kitchen and Bathroom Sanitization

Sanitary areas are deep-cleaned and disinfected to ensure safe usage immediately after handover.

### Fixture and Fitting Cleaning

Light fittings, switches, cabinets, and appliances are wiped, polished, and restored to a clean finish.

These services collectively define comprehensive **Post-construction Cleaning in Nairobi** for residential and commercial properties.
